Question 1

For the multimedia system Lynda, give an outline of what it is, its purpose and how it can be defined as a multimedia system.

Lynda is an American website that consists of educational videos and video courses. The courses are taught by industry experts in software, creative, and business skills. Originally founded in 1995 by Lynda Weinman as Lynda.com, it has now been acquired by LinkedIn in 2015 and is now a subsidiary of LinkedIn which is owned by Microsoft. Their online educational services cost users a monthly subscription of $25 and offers a premium subscription that gives users excercises and extra study material to accompany their video courses. Their mission statement is simply "to help you learn the skills you need to achieve your full potential". Therefore, the purpose of their website and platform is simply to help and assist their users by providing educational videos in order for their users to learn and develop skills. By definition, a multimedia system can be defined as any application that combines text with graphics, animation, audio, video, and/or virtual reality. When looking back at Lynda.com, their system consists of a range of multimedia data types including text, videos, audio and graphics. Beacuse of this, Lynda.com is classified as a multimedia system.
