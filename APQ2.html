Question 2

What is the difference between morphing and warping an image?

Criteria Marks
Describes what morphing is 1
Describes what warping is 1

Sample answer:
Morphing is the act of turning one image into another representing a smooth change whereas warping refers to the modification of an image by stretching and resizing parts of it whilst still making the original image recognisable.
