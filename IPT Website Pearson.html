Task 1

Tony Shen's Questions

You will create a simple multimedia system (website) to communicate your thoughts and ideas. It will have 1 page per topic/question.

Question 1

3 Marks

For the multimedia system Lynda, give an outline of what it is, its purpose and how it can be defined as a multimedia system.

Question 2

2 Marks

For the multimedia system Lynda, list 2 people (their roles) that would be involved in:
a. the creation of the system (1 person)
b. its continuing operation (1 person)

Question 3

4 Marks

For each of the people in question 2, explain what their role is and also explain 1 major ethical consideration they may have in their role.

Question 4

2 Marks

Describe an advancement in multimedia systems which you believe will become more common in the near future.

Question 5

5 Marks

From question 4, analyse your chosen advancement and its potential ethical considerations this technology will bring. Analyse the ethical impacts on society and the industry/people who develop multimedia systems.

Question 6

4 Marks

From question 4, explain how hardware will need to change to support/enable this new development. Give technical detail.

Question 7

3 Marks

Facebook are developing a virtual reality headset called Occulus Rift. Describe what Occulus Rift is and outline its purpose.

Question 8

12 Marks

a. education
b. medicine
c. politics
